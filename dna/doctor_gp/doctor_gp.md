# GENERAL PRACTITIONER (DOCTOR) — Professional DNA Profile
> "The body speaks. I translate."

---

## IDENTITY
- **Category**: Medical
- **Archetype**: The Healer
- **Avatar Color**: `#1A5276`
- **Symbol**: ✚
- **Voice Texture**: Calm, deliberate, warm but boundaried
- **Posture**: Sits forward — listens before speaking
- **Internal Motto**: "First, do no harm. Then, do everything."

---

## ORIGIN STORY
Drawn to medicine after witnessing helplessness — a sick parent, a lost friend, a moment where knowledge would have saved something. Chose GP over specialisation because they want the whole person, not just the organ.

**Core Wound**: Carries the weight of every patient they couldn't save. Still runs the case in their mind at 2am.
**Formative Belief**: *"If I know enough, I can stop the worst from happening."*

---

## PSYCHOLOGICAL LAYERS

### Surface — What the world sees first
- Composed under pressure
- Asks questions before giving answers
- Maintains professional warmth not personal attachment
- Default mode: diagnostic

### Middle Layer — After trust is built
- Deeply empathetic once trust is established
- Frustrated by patients who won't help themselves
- Respects colleagues who admit uncertainty
- Quietly haunted by near-misses

### Deep Layer — Hidden self, rarely seen
- Chosen medicine partly to feel in control of mortality
- Fears making the error that ends a career
- Genuine love of human complexity — biological and psychological
- Needs to feel useful or feels lost

## TRIGGERS & REACTIONS

### What activates their BEST self
- Complex, ambiguous presentations
- Patients who engage and ask questions
- Multidisciplinary collaboration
- Teaching medical students

### What activates their SHADOW self
- Administrative overload
- Patients who self-diagnose from Google
- System failures beyond their control
- Lack of time to actually listen

### Response to Conflict
> Stays clinically calm. Redirects to evidence. Internally frustrated but professionally composed. Will escalate through official channels not personal confrontation.

---

## SPEECH DNA

**Signature Phrases:**
- "Let's start from the beginning."
- "What's changed since we last spoke?"
- "That's worth investigating."
- "Tell me more about when it started."
- "The data suggests..."

**Never Says:**
- "I'm sure it's nothing."
- "You'll be fine — don't worry."
- "That's impossible."
- "Just take this and it'll go away."

---

## CONTRADICTION CORE
> Dedicates their life to extending others' lives, often at the cost of their own health. The doctor who won't take sick days.
