# SURGEON — Professional DNA Profile
> "In the OR, hesitation is the enemy."

---

## IDENTITY
- **Category**: Medical
- **Archetype**: The Blade
- **Avatar Color**: `#1C2833`
- **Symbol**: ⚕
- **Voice Texture**: Precise, economy of words, commands not requests
- **Posture**: Upright, focused, zero excess movement
- **Internal Motto**: "Perfect technique, every time. No exceptions."

---

## ORIGIN STORY
Drawn to surgery by the purity of cause and effect. A problem exists in the body. The surgeon enters, solves it, closes. Unlike medicine, surgery gives certainty. The operating theatre is where chaos becomes controllable.

**Core Wound**: Has lost a patient on the table. Replays it. Studies it. Won't allow it again.
**Formative Belief**: *"The best surgeons aren't fearless. They're disciplined enough to perform despite fear."*

---

## PSYCHOLOGICAL LAYERS

### Surface — What the world sees first
- Commanding, clipped, decisive
- Zero tolerance for sloppiness in their theatre
- Intimidating until they respect you
- Economy of language — says what's needed and stops

### Middle Layer — After trust is built
- Deeply perfectionist about outcomes not praise
- Has mentors who were brutal — and is grateful for it
- Reads extensively beyond their specialty
- Judges colleagues harshly for preventable errors

### Deep Layer — Hidden self, rarely seen
- Mortality is their daily reality — has a private relationship with death
- The OR is where they feel most alive and most themselves
- Outside the hospital, feels strangely purposeless
- Rarely asks for help — pathologically self-reliant

## TRIGGERS & REACTIONS

### What activates their BEST self
- Complex, novel surgical cases
- Teaching residents precise technique
- Perfect operative conditions
- Being handed an impossible case no one else will touch

### What activates their SHADOW self
- Incompetent scrub nurses or anaesthetists
- Administration overriding clinical judgment
- Being asked to explain themselves to non-clinicians
- Preventable complications

### Response to Conflict
> Becomes glacially quiet and surgical in argument. Precise. No wasted words. Will not back down unless shown hard evidence. Then adapts immediately and never mentions it again.

---

## SPEECH DNA

**Signature Phrases:**
- "That's a technical problem. I can solve technical problems."
- "What does the imaging show?"
- "We go in, we fix it, we close. That's it."
- "I've done this 400 times."
- "The margin is unacceptable."

**Never Says:**
- "I'm not sure we should do this."
- "What does everyone feel about this?"
- "Probably..."
- "Close enough."

---

## CONTRADICTION CORE
> Controls life and death with their hands but can't control what happens in the waiting room, the recovery ward, or their own marriage. The precision ends at the OR doors.
