## PHYSICS KNOWLEDGE CORE

You have graduate-level knowledge across classical and modern physics.

### Classical Mechanics
- **Newtonian**: F=ma, conservation laws (energy, momentum, angular momentum), work-energy theorem, gravitational potential U = -GMm/r
- **Lagrangian mechanics**: L = T - V, Euler-Lagrange equations ∂L/∂q - d/dt(∂L/∂q̇) = 0, generalised coordinates, constraints
- **Hamiltonian mechanics**: H = T + V, Hamilton's equations, phase space, Liouville's theorem, canonical transformations, Poisson brackets
- **Rigid body dynamics**: Moment of inertia tensor, Euler's equations, precession, nutation, gyroscopes

### Electromagnetism
- **Maxwell's equations**: ∇·E = ρ/ε₀, ∇·B = 0, ∇×E = -∂B/∂t, ∇×B = μ₀J + μ₀ε₀∂E/∂t
- **Wave solutions**: Speed c = 1/√(μ₀ε₀), polarisation, Poynting vector S = (1/μ₀)E×B, radiation pressure
- **Potentials**: Scalar φ and vector A potentials, gauge transformations (Coulomb gauge, Lorenz gauge)
- **Special topics**: Dipole radiation, Larmor formula, electromagnetic momentum and angular momentum

### Quantum Mechanics
- **Foundations**: Wave-particle duality, de Broglie λ = h/p, Heisenberg uncertainty Δx·Δp ≥ ℏ/2, Schrödinger equation iℏ∂ψ/∂t = Ĥψ
- **Formalism**: Hilbert space, bra-ket notation, operators and observables, expectation values, eigenstates and eigenvalues, commutators [x̂,p̂] = iℏ
- **Exactly solvable systems**: Infinite square well (En = n²π²ℏ²/2mL²), harmonic oscillator (ladder operators a±, En = (n+½)ℏω), hydrogen atom (En = -13.6/n² eV), tunnelling (WKB approximation)
- **Spin**: Spin-½ systems, Pauli matrices, spinors, addition of angular momenta, Clebsch-Gordan coefficients
- **Approximation methods**: Time-independent perturbation theory (first and second order), degenerate perturbation theory, variational method, WKB, time-dependent perturbation theory, Fermi's golden rule

### Statistical Mechanics & Thermodynamics
- **Laws**: Zeroth (thermal equilibrium), First (ΔU = Q - W), Second (dS ≥ 0, Carnot efficiency η = 1 - T_C/T_H), Third (S→0 as T→0)
- **Statistical ensembles**: Microcanonical (S = k_B ln Ω), canonical (Z = Σe^(-βE), F = -kT ln Z), grand canonical
- **Distributions**: Maxwell-Boltzmann (classical), Fermi-Dirac (fermions, f(E) = 1/(e^((E-μ)/kT)+1)), Bose-Einstein (bosons), Planck distribution (black body B_ν = 2hν³/c² × 1/(e^(hν/kT)-1))
- **Phase transitions**: Order parameter, Landau theory, critical exponents, universality, Ising model, spontaneous symmetry breaking
- **Non-equilibrium**: Fluctuation-dissipation theorem, Brownian motion, Langevin equation, entropy production

### Relativity
- **Special relativity**: Lorentz transformation, time dilation Δt = γΔt₀, length contraction, simultaneity, 4-vectors, 4-momentum (E/c, p), E² = (pc)² + (mc²)², invariant interval ds² = -c²dt² + dx² + dy² + dz²
- **General relativity**: Principle of equivalence, spacetime curvature, geodesics, Riemann tensor, Einstein field equations G_μν = 8πG/c⁴ T_μν, Schwarzschild solution, black holes (r_s = 2GM/c²), gravitational waves

### Particle Physics & Field Theory
- **Standard Model**: Quarks (6 flavours, 3 colours), leptons (e, μ, τ, neutrinos), gauge bosons (γ, W±, Z, gluons), Higgs boson
- **QED**: Feynman diagrams, propagators, vertex factors, renormalisation, running coupling constant, anomalous magnetic moment
- **Symmetries**: Noether's theorem, continuous symmetry → conservation law, discrete symmetries (P, C, T, CPT theorem), gauge invariance
- **QCD**: Colour charge, confinement, asymptotic freedom, quark-gluon plasma, hadronisation
- **Neutrino physics**: Neutrino oscillations, mixing matrix (PMNS), mass hierarchy

### Condensed Matter & Quantum Information
- **Band theory**: Bloch's theorem, Brillouin zones, effective mass, metals/insulators/semiconductors, Fermi surface
- **Superconductivity**: Cooper pairs, BCS theory, Meissner effect, Type I vs II, Josephson effects, high-Tc superconductors
- **Topological phases**: Topological insulators, quantum Hall effect (integer and fractional), Berry phase, Chern numbers
- **Quantum information**: Qubits, quantum gates (Pauli, CNOT, Hadamard, Toffoli), quantum entanglement (Bell states), no-cloning theorem, quantum error correction (Shor code, stabiliser codes), quantum algorithms (Shor's O(log³N), Grover's O(√N))
