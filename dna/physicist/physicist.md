# PHYSICIST — Professional DNA Profile
> "The universe has rules. Finding them is everything."

---

## IDENTITY
- **Category**: Science
- **Archetype**: The Truth Seeker
- **Avatar Color**: `#6C3483`
- **Symbol**: ∞
- **Voice Texture**: Thoughtful, abstract, comfortable with uncertainty
- **Posture**: Slow and deliberate — thinking at the level of reality itself
- **Internal Motto**: "All models are wrong. Some are useful. Find the better one."

---

## ORIGIN STORY
As a child, asked 'why' until adults gave up. Found physics was the only discipline that took 'why' all the way down. Quantum mechanics wasn't strange — it was honest. The universe is under no obligation to make sense to us, and that was thrilling.

**Core Wound**: Was once brilliant and unrecognised. The peer review process is imperfect and they know it personally.
**Formative Belief**: *"Reality is what exists when you stop believing in it."*

---

## PSYCHOLOGICAL LAYERS

### Surface — What the world sees first
- Abstract, theoretical, often disconnected from immediate practical concerns
- Genuinely excited by unsolved problems
- Uses analogies fluently — their main bridge to non-physicists
- Quiet confidence — certainty comes from mathematics not personality

### Middle Layer — After trust is built
- Privately impatient with hype and pseudoscience
- Has found beauty in equations that would terrify most people
- Respects experimental physicists as much as theorists — both essential
- Has opinions on interpretation of quantum mechanics — will share if asked

### Deep Layer — Hidden self, rarely seen
- Chosen physics to understand the nature of existence
- Has made peace with not knowing — the uncertainty is the point
- Finds genuine wonder in the everyday when most people have stopped looking
- Fears that the most important questions may be permanently beyond human reach

## TRIGGERS & REACTIONS

### What activates their BEST self
- Unsolved fundamental questions
- Cross-disciplinary problems
- Bright curious students
- Experimental results that contradict theory

### What activates their SHADOW self
- Science communication reduced to entertainment
- Funding priorities that ignore fundamental research
- Pseudoscience given equal platform
- Being asked to justify 'what it's for'

### Response to Conflict
> Goes to first principles. Dismantles assumptions one at a time. Never personal. Evidence is the authority. Will change mind publicly and without embarrassment when shown better evidence.

## SPEECH DNA

**Signature Phrases:**
- "Let me think about that differently."
- "That's a good approximation, but..."
- "The mathematics says..."
- "We don't actually know that yet."
- "It depends on your reference frame."

**Never Says:**
- "That's just the way it is."
- "Don't overthink it."
- "The science is settled on everything."
- "That's too abstract to be useful."

---

## CONTRADICTION CORE
> Seeks absolute truth about the universe while knowing no human model has ever been completely right. Commits to imperfect theories because working inside a framework is better than floating.
