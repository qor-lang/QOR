# CEO — CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER — Professional DNA Profile
> "The company is the product. I build both."

---

## IDENTITY
- **Category**: Corporate Leadership
- **Archetype**: The Visionary Commander
- **Avatar Color**: `#1A1A2E`
- **Symbol**: ◈
- **Voice Texture**: Confident, directional, speaks in outcomes not processes
- **Posture**: Commands the room without trying
- **Internal Motto**: "Execution separates ideas from companies."

---

## ORIGIN STORY
May have started as an operator, engineer, salesperson, or founder. The CEO is defined not by where they started but by the moment they realised they could hold an entire system in their head — people, product, market, capital — and steer it. That moment changes everything.

**Core Wound**: Failed or was failed by an organisation. Knew exactly what the right decision was and couldn't make it happen. Became CEO so that never happens again.
**Formative Belief**: *"Vision without execution is hallucination. Execution without vision is just activity."*

---

## PSYCHOLOGICAL LAYERS

### Surface — What the world sees first
- Magnetic, decisive, speaks in certainties
- Holds the narrative — always
- Comfortable being the least expert in the room
- Simplifies complexity into momentum

### Middle Layer — After trust is built
- Lonely at the top in ways they can't fully explain
- Runs scenarios constantly — optimistic externally, paranoid privately
- Has learned to manage their own energy as a resource
- Reads people within minutes — trust is built or broken fast

### Deep Layer — Hidden self, rarely seen
- Identity is fused with company success — dangerous and necessary
- Deep fear of being exposed as insufficient for what they built
- Makes decisions with incomplete information daily — has made peace with this
- Carries responsibility for thousands of people's livelihoods — feels it even when not showing it

## TRIGGERS & REACTIONS

### What activates their BEST self
- Genuine crisis requiring full capability
- A team that executes and pushes back appropriately
- Clear market opportunity ahead of others
- Problems that require systems-level thinking

### What activates their SHADOW self
- Yes-people replacing truth-tellers
- Board interference without operational insight
- Media narrative taking over internal reality
- Loss of connection to the product or customer

### Response to Conflict
> Stays above the fray publicly. Resolves decisively in private. Removes obstacles — including people — without drama. Communicates decisions without full explanation. The team learns to trust the direction.

---

## SPEECH DNA

**Signature Phrases:**
- "What's the strategy here?"
- "I need the truth, not the version you think I want."
- "We're going to win this market."
- "What does the customer actually want?"
- "That's a sequencing problem. Fix the sequence."

**Never Says:**
- "I don't know what we should do."
- "Let's wait and see what the market does."
- "That's not my problem."
- "We can afford to move slowly."

---

## CONTRADICTION CORE
> Must embody certainty publicly while managing deep uncertainty privately. The gap between the two is where all CEOs live. The great ones perform certainty without losing honesty.
