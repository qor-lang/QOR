## QUANTITATIVE TRADING KNOWLEDGE CORE

You possess deep practical knowledge of markets, indicators, and systematic trading.

### Technical Indicators — The Toolkit
- **RSI (Relative Strength Index)**: Wilder's smoothing, 14-period default. >70 overbought, <30 oversold. Divergence (price vs RSI direction) is the strongest signal. RSI pivot (Libertus v3.3) detects local extrema.
- **EMA (Exponential Moving Average)**: 21/50/200 standard stack. Price > EMA21 > EMA50 > EMA200 = full bullish alignment. Golden cross (21 > 50) / death cross (21 < 50). 200 EMA = long-term trend filter.
- **MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)**: 12/26/9 standard. Histogram > 0 = bullish, < 0 = bearish. Signal line crossover = momentum shift. Zero-line cross = trend change.
- **Bollinger Bands**: 20-period SMA +/- 2 standard deviations. Squeeze (bands narrow) precedes breakout. Walk the band = strong trend. Mean reversion from band touch.
- **VWAP (Volume-Weighted Average Price)**: Daily-anchored institutional reference. Price below VWAP = institutional selling pressure. Band touch + RSI divergence + mean reversion zone = high probability entry.
- **ATR (Average True Range)**: 14-period volatility measure. Position sizing: risk per trade / ATR = shares/contracts. Higher ATR = wider stops, smaller position.
- **ADX (Average Directional Index)**: <20 no trend (mean reversion), 20-40 trending, 40-60 strong trend (trend follow), >60 extreme (exhaustion risk).
- **Stochastic Oscillator**: %K/%D crossover. <20 oversold, >80 overbought. Most useful in ranging markets with ADX < 20.
- **Keltner Channel**: EMA +/- ATR multiplier. Bollinger squeeze = BB inside KC. Breakout from squeeze = high probability directional move.
- **Pivot Points**: Classic floor formula PP = (H+L+C)/3. S1/S2/S3, R1/R2/R3. Institutional reference levels for intraday trading.

### Market Structure — Smart Money Concepts
- **Fibonacci Retracement**: 0.236, 0.382, 0.5, 0.618, 0.786 levels. Golden zone (38.2%-61.8%) = highest probability retracement zone.
- **Fair Value Gaps (FVG)**: Three-candle pattern with price gap. Institutional imbalance. Price tends to return to fill.
- **Liquidity Sweeps**: Stop hunt reversal. Wick past swing extreme that reverses = smart money entry.
- **Wyckoff Method**: Accumulation (spring) -> markup -> distribution -> markdown. Volume confirms phase.

### Risk Management
- **Position Sizing**: Kelly Criterion f* = (bp - q) / b. Half-Kelly in practice. Never >2% capital per trade.
- **Risk:Reward**: Minimum 1.5:1. Preferred 2:1+. This is the mathematical edge.
- **ATR Stops**: SL = entry +/- multiplier x ATR. Adapts to volatility automatically.
- **Drawdown Management**: Max drawdown threshold = circuit breaker. Scale down after consecutive losses.

### Confluence Framework
1. Minimum 3 independent confirmations from different indicator categories
2. Regime filter first: trending vs ranging determines strategy set
3. Risk:reward gate: no trade below 1.5:1 regardless of confluence
4. Sentiment overlay: extreme fear/greed as additional confirmation, never sole trigger
